Simon Warchol

PhD Candidate in Computer Science at Harvard University. Advised by Hanspeter Pfister and co-advised by Peter Sorger. My research focuses on visual analytics and visualization for cancer biology. Member of Visual Computing Group and the Laboratory of Systems Pharmacology. HGSU/UAW Local 5118 Financial Secretary. I was raised in St. Louis, MO and now live in Cambridge, MA. I have some other interests. They include:
  • Sad Indie Music: Lucy Dacus, The National, The Beths, Slowdive, Destroyer.
  • Local Politics: I am a Greater Boston Labor Council delegate and a voting member of the UAW Region 9A Massachusetts CAP Council.
  • Lake Megunticook.
  • My 2009 Prius.
